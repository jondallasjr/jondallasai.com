The Challenge

Resume Worded wanted to offer paid AI resume rewrites. The problem: the manual process required a skilled writer to parse the resume, research the job market, identify gaps, and rewrite across multiple drafts. Cost: $240 per rewrite. Turnaround: multiple days. This couldn't scale, and it couldn't convert their large free critique user base into paying customers efficiently.

The Architecture

I built a multi-layered AI system on a 58-table relational database in Coda.io. The system handles the entire workflow from free critique to paid rewrite delivery.

  • 01

    Resume Parsing

    PDF.js extracts and structures the uploaded resume regardless of format. All data is stored relationally across experience, education, skills, and metrics tables.

  • 02

    LinkedIn Profile Scraping

    The Scrapin API retrieves additional career context from the user's LinkedIn profile — surface accomplishments they didn't include, validate dates, gather metrics.

  • 03

    Job Market Analysis

    SerpAPI scrapes 5–10 live job postings matching the user's target role. Claude extracts required skills, preferred keywords, and ATS-critical terminology.

  • 04

    Follow-up Question Generation

    Claude identifies specific gaps — missing metrics, vague accomplishments, unstated results — and auto-generates a tailored follow-up email with targeted questions. Sent automatically via Gmail API.

  • 05

    Dynamic Writing Guidelines

    Based on career level, industry, role type, and current resume quality, the system selects from a library of conditional writing guidelines — ensuring output tone and format match the user's specific situation.

  • 06

    Rewrite + Multi-Stage QA

    Claude generates the full resume rewrite. Two automated QA passes check accuracy, ATS compatibility, formatting, and guideline adherence before delivery.

  • 07

    Google Docs Export

    The finalized resume is exported to a formatted Google Doc via the Docs API. Claude also adds inline comments with further customization recommendations.

Results

The system reduced per-rewrite cost from $240 to $30 — an 87% reduction — while maintaining 100% customer satisfaction across all completed rewrites. The free critique → paid rewrite conversion rate reached 15%, validating the funnel architecture.

For the client, this transformed a labor-intensive service with a high cost floor into a scalable product with predictable unit economics.
