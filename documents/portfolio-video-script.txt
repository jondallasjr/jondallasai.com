Resume Bean: AI-Powered Resume Critique & Rewrite Platform
Overview
Resume Bean is a fully automated, multi-layered AI system that revolutionizes the resume review and optimization process. The platform provides free resume critiques with actionable insights and offers paid, comprehensive resume rewrites—achieving a 15% conversion rate from free to paid services with 100% customer satisfaction across 30+ resumes.
Business Impact
87% Cost Reduction: Decreased internal costs per resume rewrite from $240 to $30
15% Conversion Rate: Successfully converted free critique users to paid rewrite customers
100% Customer Satisfaction: Perfect satisfaction rate across all completed rewrites
Operational Efficiency: Dramatically reduced manual effort required for resume analysis and rewriting
Technical Implementation
The system employs a sophisticated 58-table relational database architecture that orchestrates a complex workflow:
Data Collection & Analysis
Resume Parsing Engine: Automatically extracts and structures data from uploaded resumes regardless of format
LinkedIn Profile Scraper: Gathers supplementary career information to identify missing achievements and experiences
Job Posting Analysis: Scrapes relevant job postings based on target roles to identify critical skills and qualifications
ATS Keyword Determination: Identifies applicant tracking system (ATS) keywords essential for resume visibility
Intelligent Processing
Context-Aware Prompt Chaining: Applies specific writing guidelines based on career level, industry, job relevance, and current resume quality
Dynamic Follow-Up Generation: Automatically creates tailored questionnaires to gather missing metrics and achievements
Output Generation
Tailored Content Creation: Generates optimized content that balances ATS requirements with human readability
Multi-Stage QA Process: Performs automated quality assurance checks for accuracy, formatting, and writing guideline adherence
Google Docs Integration: Seamlessly exports finalized resumes with embedded comments suggesting further customizations
Tech Stack
AI/ML: OpenAI & Anthropic APIs (Claude) for content generation and analysis
Backend: Coda.io for low-code database architecture and workflow management
Document Processing: PDF.js for resume parsing and extraction
Data Collection: Custom web scrapers for LinkedIn profiles and job postings
APIs: Google Docs API for document generation and delivery
Workflow Visualization
Initial Submission: User uploads resume and completes basic questionnaire
Free Critique Generation: System analyzes resume structure, content, and ATS compatibility
Conversion: User receives free critique with option to purchase full rewrite service
Enhanced Data Collection: For paid customers, system scrapes additional information and generates follow-up questions
Intelligent Rewrite: System applies appropriate writing guidelines based on user profile and job targets
Delivery & Feedback: User receives optimized resume with embedded improvement recommendations
Future Development
Integration of real-time job market analytics to provide industry-specific optimization tips
Enhanced visualization of "before/after" metrics to better illustrate improvements
Development of an interview preparation module based on the optimized resume content

KEY LEARNINGS
EEAT Integration is Critical
Proprietary Data Creates Unique Value
Low-Code Prototyping Accelerates Development
AI Systems Need Human Oversight
Structured Output Formats Improve Reliability
EEAT Integration is Critical: Google's emphasis on Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness has made generic AI-generated content less effective, necessitating systems that deliberately incorporate these signals
Proprietary Data Creates Unique Value: The most valuable content combines publicly available information with unique insights only available to the client
Low-Code Prototyping Accelerates Development: Building the initial proof-of-concept in Coda.io allowed for rapid iteration and validation before committing to full production development
AI Systems Need Human Oversight: While the system automates much of the content creation process, the final human approval step remains essential for quality control
Structured Output Formats Improve Reliability: Having Claude return responses in JSON format significantly improved parsing reliability and system stability
